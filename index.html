South Africa

South Africa is a diverse and vibrant country located at the southern tip of the African continent, known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and complex history. It is often called the “Rainbow Nation” because of its multicultural population and 11 official languages. South Africa boasts some of the worlds most iconic natural landmarks, such as Table Mountain, Kruger National Park, and the Cape of Good Hope.
